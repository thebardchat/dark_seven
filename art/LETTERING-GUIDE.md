## THE VOICES (only two carry the story)

| Tag | Who | Box style |
|-----|-----|-----------|
| **THE MAN** | the nameless detective, first person | plain caption box, bone on black |
| **THE DARK** | the phantom's voice — quiet, almost kind | **torn-edged box with red dripping from one corner** |
| **ARC** / **WELD** | shadow-selves of his own mind | plain box, slightly greyed |
| **SFX** | sound | hand-lettered in the art, no box |

**Rule:** THE MAN and THE DARK never share a box. When a page inverts (Cut 4), set the caption so it **reads correctly when the reader rotates the book.**

4. Rename finals from `regen_*` to their page code (e.g. `PAGE-K_shield.png`) when locked.
