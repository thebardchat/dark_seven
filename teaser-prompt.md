# DARK SEVEN — Teaser / Title-Card Recipe
*volume-three/teaser-prompt.md · the recipe for the neon-cross-and-blood loop, and every teaser like it*

---

## What it is

The opening **teaser / title-card loop** for DARK SEVEN — Volume Three. The whole thesis of the book in about ten seconds: **grace lights up, blood covers it, it drains to a black void, and grace comes back.** The Devil does not win, even in the trailer.

Built in **Gemini (video / Flash)** by attaching Shane's actual Celtic cross image so the shape stays exact. Sound generated in-model — the electrical hum and neon buzz are part of it.

**The five beats of the loop:**
1. The cross **ignites like a broken neon sign** — flickers, fails to catch, sparks, a lightning strike, then surges to a steady white glow.
2. It **holds, lit and glowing** — Shane's exact cross (flared arms, double ring, four-point star, long flared shaft).
3. **Thick blood pours down and completely covers it**, drowning the cross in red; the glow dies.
4. The blood **runs down and drains off** while **pure black fills from the top**, until the frame is empty black — the void.
5. The cross **reignites** like the neon powering back on, and it **loops seamlessly.**

---

## THE RULE — locked, non-negotiable

- **Three colors only: pure black, clean bright WHITE, and bold pure BLOOD RED.** Nothing else, ever.
- **The blood is vivid saturated crimson — never brown, maroon, rust, chocolate, or dark red.**
- **The glow/cross is clean white or that same red — never cream, gold, amber, yellow, or beige.**
- **The cross shape is never redrawn.** Attach the reference image every time and tell it not to alter the design.

---

## How to make it

1. **Attach the clean cross image** (the vector — IMG_8572).
2. Paste the **generation prompt** below.
3. If the color drifts warm (it will try to), paste the **color-lock correction.**
4. Repeat the correction until the color is clean. Everything else usually holds.

## GENERATION PROMPT

```
Using the EXACT Celtic cross in the attached image (keep its shape, ring,
four-pointed star, and flared shaft precisely — do NOT redraw it), animate a
seamless 10-second loop on a PURE BLACK background. Colors: ONLY black, bright
white, and bold pure blood red. No green, no gold, no other colors.

Follow these five distinct beats with clear, separated timing:
0.0-2.5s — the cross is DARK, then IGNITES like a broken neon sign: it
  flickers, stutters, fails to catch two or three times with small electric
  sparks and a lightning arc, then surges to a full steady white glow.
2.5-4.0s — it holds, glowing steadily.
4.0-6.5s — thick glossy BLOOD RED liquid pours from the top and FULLY COVERS
  the entire cross until it is completely hidden under red; the glow dies out.
6.5-8.5s — the blood runs down and drains off the bottom in dripping strands
  while PURE BLACK fills from the top, until the frame is 100% SOLID BLACK and
  EMPTY — no cross visible at all.
8.5-10s — the cross flickers back to life like the neon reigniting, matching
  the very first frame so the loop repeats seamlessly.

The cross NEVER changes shape — only light and blood move over it. High-contrast
film noir, grain, dark vignette. Add a low electrical hum and neon-buzz sound
with the flicker. Portrait. Seamless loop.
```

## COLOR-LOCK CORRECTION *(paste if it goes brown / gold)*

```
Keep everything exactly as it is — the animation, timing, sound, flicker, and
cross shape are all perfect. Change ONLY the colors. Use strictly three colors
and nothing else: pure black, clean bright WHITE, and BOLD PURE BLOOD RED — a
vivid, saturated crimson like fresh blood.

The blood must NOT be brown, maroon, rust, chocolate, or dark red. Make it
bright, glossy, saturated blood red.

The cross and its glow must be clean WHITE or that same bold red — never cream,
gold, amber, yellow, or beige.

Pure black background. Absolutely no other colors anywhere in the frame.
```

## WHAT WE LEARNED *(so it lands faster next time)*

- **Gemini defaults to warm tones** — cream/gold glow, brown/maroon blood. You have to **name the wrong colors** ("not brown, maroon, rust; never cream, gold, yellow") to push it off them. Saying "blood red" alone isn't enough.
- **"Five distinct beats" with explicit timestamps** forces the phased loop. Without it, Gemini smears everything into one continuous bleed.
- **"COMPLETELY hidden" and "100% SOLID BLACK and EMPTY"** are what force the void beat — the empty-black moment where grace is gone before it returns. That beat is the heartbeat; protect it.
- **Attach the image + "do NOT redraw"** is what keeps the cross shape exact. Generating from text alone always drifts to a generic cross.
- **The sound sells it.** The low electrical hum + neon buzz under the flicker is the funeral-neon mood. Ask for it explicitly.
- Model: **Gemini Flash (video).** ~10s, portrait, seamless loop.

## FOR THE OTHER TRACKS

Same recipe, swap the center. Any DARK SEVEN teaser can use this exact structure — **neon ignite → hold → blood engulf → drain to black void → reignite → loop** — with a different central image (the shield, the 7, a track's key object). Keep the three-color rule and the same color-lock language every time. The bones don't change; only what's at the center does.
