The Author

Dispatch
Manager.
Builder.

Shane Brazelton is a concrete dispatch manager in North Alabama who built a publishing operation on a Raspberry Pi in a closet. Nobody asked him to. He did it anyway.

He records his books during the pre-dawn commute — raw, stream-of-consciousness, voice-to-text — and presses them into noir. The machine he co-built does the shaping. The voice is his.

Dark but not hopeless. Cynical but anchored. The Devil does not win here.
